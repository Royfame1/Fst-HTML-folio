The Best Movies According to Roland

My Top Four (3) Movies of All Time

Legend of The Seeker

This is a very interesting Adventurous mobie with magical work at its peak

State Of Emergency

This is a Nigerian Home movie with a lot of suspends. Betrayal, Patriotism, and sacrifice can be link to the theme

The Senator

From his High School days, he dreamt of being a senator. Very intelligent and humble yet he won't let anyone stand on his way of becoming a senator. He deployed both the ugly and fair means of becoming one.
